In 2024, my family took me on my first trip to Thailand. My uncle had visited before, so he showed us many great places to eat and explore. Athough I have a peanut allergy, I was still able to enjoy the diverse and delicious Thai cuisine. I was especially drawn to the bustling cities and the stray cats roaming freely throughout the streets. *Disclaimer: The video says 2025 because I posted it in 2025.
